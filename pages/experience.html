The Ritual.

A scent that has never existed, composed around a body that has never existed before. Here is what unfolds inside the atelier — minute by minute, sense by sense.

00 · 0–10 min

Arrival.

A concierge greets you at the boutique. The atelier door is unmarked except for a small NFC plaque and a single brass arc of light. Inside: charred oak, rust velvet, ambient 36°C warmth.

You are offered jasmine tea and a one-page consent visualisation explaining exactly which signals will be read, where they live, and when they are erased. You sign on glass. The lights dim by one stop.

01 · 10–35 min

Baseline.

You settle into the ScentPod. The neuroheadset is fitted in under 90 seconds — soft contact, no gel, dry electrodes. Your wrist rests on the sensing capsule.

Over twelve minutes the system reads skin pH, temperature, sebum, hydration and your unique ambient VOC signature, while a curated palette of 24 anchor accords is presented one at a time. EEG captures emotional valence and arousal for each — not opinion, biology.

The atelier perfumer watches the build with you, asking three plain questions: where do you wear scent, when, and for whom. Nothing else. Your map appears on the table — luminous, abstract, yours.

02 · 35–75 min

Composition.

Perso micro-dispenses your first 0.5 ml sample. You smell. You speak — "more luminous", "less green", "warmer in the dry-down". The system adjusts. You smell again.

Most compositions resolve in two to three iterations. The perfumer steers, naming what you cannot — bergamot oil pressed late, an iris butter from Florence, a single note of saffron tincture. The AI proposes; the human refines.

When you nod, the formula is locked. A 30-second silence follows by tradition: the first wear of an unprecedented scent.

03 · 75–90 min

Bottling.

50 ml is dispensed into the YSL Atelier flacon — heavy weighted glass, brushed gold cap, an NFC chip set into the base. You name your scent (or refuse to — the most beautiful thing the system has heard is "I have no name for this yet").

An engraver finishes the bottle while you walk a final wear-test through the boutique. Your ScentID vault is provisioned. A ceramic refill card is placed in your hand.

All session biometrics are erased in front of you. Only the formula vector and the bottle remain.

Aftercare

What happens
after the atelier.

The bottle is the beginning, not the end. Your ScentID becomes a quiet companion — for refills, evolutions, and one annual reflection.

Day 0–7

Skin marriage.

A fragrance composed to your biology evolves over its first seven days on skin as alcohol, water and fixatives settle. We send a daily wear-note from the perfumer — descriptive, not promotional.

Refill · 90 days

Tap to refill.

Tap the bottle base on any YSL Atelier or refill counter to order. Same formula, or — if you wish — a Scent Evolution recomposed to a fresh micro-baseline at season change or major life event.

Annual

The Reflection.

Once a year, you are invited to a 30-minute reflection — a brief baseline read against your original. Most clients evolve subtly. Some compose entirely new chapters. The ScentID vault remembers them all.

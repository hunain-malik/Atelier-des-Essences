Platform deep dive

Six platforms.
Production-grade.

Each component below is either commercially deployed today or lives inside L'Oréal's R&D pipeline. Atelier integration is the white space — not the science itself.

01 / Neural Sensing

YSL Scent-Sation

Live · Production

A 16-channel dry-electrode EEG headset jointly engineered by L'Oréal Tech Incubator, EMOTIV and Oxford EEF research. Deployed in YSL flagship Dubai Mall since 2024 for in-store fragrance recommendation.

Atelier extension: we route the same emotional valence + arousal signal into the formulation engine instead of the recommendation engine — converting "what should you buy" into "what should we compose".

02 / Olfactive Capture

Osmobloom™

Adapted to skin

L'Oréal's waterless air-capture technology, originally developed to extract VOC molecules from living flowers without harm or solvent. Used today in Lancôme La Vie Est Belle.

Atelier extension: miniaturised inside ScentPod to capture the client's own ambient skin VOC — your unique molecular signature feeds the composition. Patentable.

03 / Formulation Hardware

Perso

Repurposed for scent

L'Oréal's at-home AI micro-dispensing platform for personalised cosmetics. Won Innovation Award at CES 2020 with the YSL Rouge Sur Mesure variant.

Atelier extension: reconfigured for fragrance — 24 anchor accord cartridges + dilution stage + alcohol carrier. Sample volume calibrated to 0.5 ml for iterative composition.

04 / AI Platform

ModiFace

Shared infrastructure

L'Oréal's AI/data platform powering 30+ brands across virtual try-on, skin analysis and personalisation. Acquired 2018, fully integrated.

Atelier extension: the cross-brand inference layer — every Atelier session globally informs the same accord-prediction model. The flywheel is the moat.

05 / Biology Reference

Cell BioPrint

Integrated

L'Oréal's microfluidic biological profiling platform, developed for skin diagnostics and dermo-cosmetic personalisation.

Atelier extension: provides the chemistry baselining science used inside ScentPod — pH, sebum, lipid layer reading. Transforms fragrance from cosmetic veneer to biological response.

06 / Accessibility

HAPTA

Hardware reference

L'Oréal's motion-stabilised assistive applicator for users with limited hand mobility. Won CES Innovation Award 2023.

Atelier extension: the booth's ergonomic principles draw on HAPTA — wheelchair clearance, chin-rest stability, NFC-to-audio narration, braille engraving. Accessibility is structural to the product, not added on.

Patentable white spaces

Four frontiers
no competitor touches.

The components are L'Oréal's. The integration is the IP. We have identified four un-patented spaces inside fragrance that this stack opens.

White space 01

Body-VOC → formula

No fragrance brand in market reads the wearer's own VOC signature and uses it as a composition input. The combination of Osmobloom on skin + a generative accord engine is unprecedented.

White space 02

Closed-loop neural feedback

EEG-informed iterative composition — the system proposes, reads response, refines, in 60-second loops. No precedent in beauty consumer experience.

White space 03

Integrated booth system

A single sealed environment combining sensing, AI, dispensing and bottling at retail. No fragrance house ships an end-to-end booth product.

White space 04

EEG-guided creation vs. recommendation

The conceptual leap from "EEG suggests an existing scent" to "EEG composes a new scent" is itself defensible IP — the use of neural data for generative formulation is unfiled.

Privacy by architecture

Your biology never leaves the booth.

Neural and skin biometrics are processed entirely on-device inside ScentPod. The session ends with a verifiable wipe — visible to you on the console. Only an anonymous 1,024-dimensional formula vector, unlinked from PII, is stored against your bottle's NFC ID.

The system is GDPR, UAE PDPL and CCPA-aligned by design, and audited annually by an independent privacy ethics board chaired outside L'Oréal. You can request full deletion at any moment, in any market.

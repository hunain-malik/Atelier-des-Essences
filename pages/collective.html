The Collective.

The truths revealed by thousands of bespoke compositions, distilled into a single annual fragrance. Born from biometric data, finished by a master perfumer. Limited release. Refillable. $100–150.

Data as oil

A democratised
luxury layer.

Every Atelier session feeds an anonymous, aggregated insight layer inside the ScentID vault. Once a year, the perfumery team studies the resulting accord topology — what combinations are resonating across cities, biologies, generations.

From that signal we release a single edition, composed by a master perfumer, produced only to known demand. No batch forecasting. No unsold inventory. Genderless by composition. Refillable for life.

Methodology

How a Collective
edition is born.

Aggregated, anonymised, uncoupled from any individual. The Collective is a portrait of a year, not a person.

  1. 01

    Anonymous aggregation.

    Each Atelier session contributes only its 1,024-dimensional formula vector — never names, never PII. Aggregation occurs across at least 500 sessions per insight cluster.

  2. 02

    Topology mapping.

    ModiFace's clustering layer identifies the strongest accord convergences of the year — the silent agreements that biology has reached across cities and ages.

  3. 03

    Master composition.

    A guest master perfumer is briefed on the topology and given complete artistic freedom to interpret it. The AI is the brief; the human is the composer.

  4. 04

    Pre-sold release.

    The edition is released to ScentID vault holders first, on a 30-day pre-order window. Production is confirmed only against actual demand — no surplus.

The composer

Anne Flipo

Master perfumer at IFF, with thirty years composing for the houses of Yves Saint Laurent, Lancôme and Givenchy. The Collective is the first commission in beauty history to brief a master nose with aggregated neural data.

"The data did not tell me what to write. It told me what the year was already saying — and asked me to listen."

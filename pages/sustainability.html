Four commitments

Sustainability is
structural, not story.

We have engineered four hard constraints into the product so that the marketing claim is unnecessary — the architecture itself is the claim.

01 · Bottle

One bottle, for life.

Heavy weighted Italian glass, brushed-gold zinc cap. Designed for a 30-year service life with a refill cycle of 6–9 months. Replaceable atomiser only. NFC chip is recoverable on retire.

02 · Production

Made-to-order, never to-shelf.

Bespoke is composed live in front of you. Collective editions are pre-sold to ScentID vault holders for 30 days before any production batch is committed. There is no surplus to destroy because there is no surplus to make.

03 · Ingredients

Waterless. Solvent-free. Living.

Osmobloom™ air-capture means flowers contribute their molecules without being cut, distilled or extracted with hexane. Combined with L'Oréal's biotech ingredient library, we target 95% bio-based / circular by 2030.

04 · Hardware

Modular, repairable, recoverable.

Booth modules are modular and field-serviceable. Sensors, cartridges, dispensing heads — each is independently replaceable. End-of-life take-back is contracted at install.

05 · Travel

Local refill, global belonging.

Your formula travels, your bottle does not need to. Refills are composed locally at any flagship — eliminating the ship-the-product carbon cost typical of luxury distribution.

06 · Disclosure

Annual transparency report.

From Year 1 we publish ingredient origin, hardware carbon, refill rate and ScentID vault statistics openly — third-party verified by SGS. The data is the proof.

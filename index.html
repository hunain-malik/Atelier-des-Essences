The Ritual · 90 minutes

Four movements,
one essence.

Each session is a private composition. From neural baseline to bottle, every step is engineered around the singular biology of one person.

01

Opt-in &
Consent

You arrive for your appointment. Session data is ephemeral — biometrics are processed in real time and erased after formula creation. Only your anonymised ScentID vault is stored.

Privacy-first
02

Sense &
Scan

You wear the YSL Scent-Sation neuroheadset (EEG) and rest your wrist on the ScentPod — a soft sensing capsule that reads pH, temperature, sebum, moisture and your natural VOC signature via Osmobloom™ air-capture.

Biometric baseline
03

Create &
Iterate

AI synthesises a first 0.5 ml micro-sample using accord families calibrated to your neural responses and skin profile. You smell, give verbal feedback, and the formula is adjusted 2–3 times until approval.

Live co-creation
04

Bottle &
ScentID

Your finalised 50 ml formula is dispensed, bottled and engraved. The NFC-embedded refillable bottle links to your ScentID vault — instant refills or seasonal Scent Evolutions at any YSL flagship globally.

Forever yours

The Atelier Advantage

Built on three
uncompromised principles.

The first consumer-facing neural-to-formula pipeline in beauty, designed for circular luxury, and inclusive by architecture — not afterthought.

Innovative

Creation, not recommendation.

  • Neural-to-formula pipeline

    YSL Scent-Sation already recommends existing fragrances. We use the same EEG feedback to compose a new formula in real time — a first.

  • ScentPod wrist sensor

    A soft capsule reads skin pH, sebum, moisture and VOC signature via miniaturised e-nose. The wrist is where perfume lives — scientifically optimal.

  • Osmobloom™ on skin

    The waterless, solvent-free air-capture behind Osmobloom™ adapted to extract each client's unique ambient scent molecules.

  • Four unpatented white spaces

    Body-VOC → formula. Closed-loop neural feedback. Integrated booth. EEG-guided creation. All patentable. No competitor touches this.

Sustainable

Refill-first, always.

  • One bottle, for life

    NFC-embedded refillable bottle. Each refill saves ~200–250 g of packaging. Aligned to L'Oréal Luxe's 1,621% increase in refillable references.

  • Zero overproduction

    On-demand creation produces only what is ordered. No batch forecasting, no unsold inventory, no surplus destruction.

  • Osmobloom™ ingredients

    Waterless air-capture, no heat or solvents, flowers left intact. Aligned to L'Oréal's 95% biobased / circular target by 2030.

  • Data drives less waste industry-wide

    Aggregated preference data informs limited Collective releases — produced only to known demand, eliminating the industry's 95% single-use packaging norm.

Inclusive

Genderless by default.

  • Composed around chemistry, not archetype

    AI creates from individual body chemistry and neural response — never gendered marketing personas. 60% of Gen Z prefer gender-neutral fragrance.

  • Tiered access model

    Free ScentID quiz → $100–150 Collective annual edition → $400–500 full bespoke → $260–350 refill. Luxury made approachable.

  • YSL Collective — data as oil

    Aggregated insights from thousands of sessions generate limited mass-market releases. Data becomes democratised luxury.

  • Accessible by design

    HAPTA-inspired booth: wheelchair accessible, NFC-to-audio for the visually impaired, braille engraving, alcohol-free format, multi-language AI.

Considered Questions

What you might
wish to know.

A composition this intimate naturally invites questions — about biology, privacy, longevity. Answers below; the team in atelier will go deeper still.

What exactly does the EEG measure?
A 16-channel dry-electrode neuroheadset (built on EMOTIV / EEF) reads broad emotional valence and arousal — not thoughts. It is the same underlying technology already in commercial use at YSL flagship Dubai for fragrance recommendation. We extend it from suggesting existing scents to composing new ones.
How is my biometric data handled?
Session data is ephemeral — neural and skin biometrics are processed in real time and erased after formula creation. Only an anonymised ScentID vault is retained, encrypted and unlinked from personally identifying information. You can request deletion at any time, in any market, in compliance with GDPR, UAE PDPL and California CCPA.
How long does a session take?
A complete bespoke session is approximately 90 minutes — 10 minutes onboarding and consent, 25 minutes neural and biometric baseline, 40 minutes co-creation across 2–3 micro-sample iterations, and 15 minutes for engraving, ScentID vault setup and aftercare guidance.
Can I refill anywhere in the world?
Yes. Your NFC-embedded bottle and ScentID vault are recognised at every YSL flagship and Atelier-equipped travel-retail location. From Year 2 onward, remote refill ordering is also available — your formula is composed locally to the same neural and skin baseline that created the original.
What if my body chemistry changes over time?
It will — and that is the point. Each refill can be ordered as the original formula or as a Scent Evolution: a recomposition tuned to your current state at a fresh micro-baseline. We expect 20%+ of users to opt for evolutions, especially across seasons or major life moments.
Is the booth accessible?
Yes. The booth's ergonomic design draws on L'Oréal's HAPTA platform: wheelchair-accessible entry and console height, NFC-to-audio narration for visually impaired guests, braille engraving on the bottle, alcohol-free formula option, and multi-language AI conversation. Accessibility is structural, not optional.
Does the formula contain animal-derived ingredients?
No. The Atelier formula library is fully vegan, IFRA-compliant and uses Osmobloom™ air-captured naturals plus L'Oréal's library of biotech and circular synthetic accords. Ingredient transparency is available digitally via tap-to-read NFC.
